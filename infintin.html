Pioneering Technology

Our Vision

To enable sustainable mobility and industrial automation by delivering high-efficiency motors that do not depend on rare earth materials. We are reimagining electric propulsion through rare-earth-free motor design tailored for Indian operating conditions.

Our Approach

Our approach reduces dependence on resource-constrained materials and optimizes design to deliver high-performance, environmentally responsible powertrains.

Our Commitment

To achieve this, we are establishing a product and research hub for traction motor development, deeply aligned with the national imperatives of "Make in India" and "Atmanirbhar Bharat."
